How it works

Limits reset daily

This is the most you can send with PayUp or Local Transfer each day. Each method has its own limit, and both reset every day at midnight. If you're close to reaching your PayUp limit, we'll ask for your authenticator code before completing the transfer. You'll also need your authenticator code whenever you change either limit.
